What I Do

Our Service Given Physio Therapy By Expert

Expert physiotherapy services tailored to your unique needs for optimal recovery and wellness.!

Pediatric Physiotherapy

Pediatric physiotherapy focuses on improving movement and functional abilities in children with developmental, neurological, or physical challenges.

Kegel Exercises

Kegel exercises involve repeatedly contracting and relaxing the pelvic floor muscles to strengthen them and improve bladder control.

Sports Physiotherapy

Sports physiotherapy specializes in the assessment, treatment, and prevention of injuries related to sports and physical activity, enhancing performance and recovery.

Neuro Physiotherapy

Neuro physiotherapy focuses on rehabilitating individuals with neurological conditions, such as stroke or Parkinson's disease, to improve movement, function, and quality of life.

Cervical Spondylosis Physiotherapy

Cervical spondylosis physiotherapy involves targeted exercises and treatments to alleviate neck pain, improve mobility, and strengthen surrounding muscles in individuals with age-related cervical spine degeneration.

Orthopaedic Physiotherapy

Orthopaedic physiotherapy focuses on diagnosing, treating, and rehabilitating musculoskeletal injuries and conditions to restore function and mobility.

Sports Massage Therapy

Sports massage therapy uses targeted techniques to relieve muscle tension, enhance performance, and aid recovery in athletes and active individuals.

Rheumatoid Arthritis Physiotherapy

Rheumatoid arthritis physiotherapy aims to reduce pain, improve joint function, and enhance overall mobility through tailored exercises and therapeutic techniques.

About Us



Dr.Ajeeta Goel holds a Master's in Sports Physiotherapy from Amity University, where she excelled in rigorous hands-on training with the Sports Authority of India and was the top student in her class. She completed her Bachelor's degree at GGSIPU and has gained valuable experience working in various hospitals.

My professional journey includes associations with the Badminton Association of India and the Boxing Federation of India

Throughout my career, I has treated numerous Indian athletes across diverse sports, including squash, badminton, boxing, gymnastics, cycling, and tennis. Ajeeta is skilled in manual therapy and has specialized training in dry needling from South Africa, kinesiology taping from the USA, and dynamic taping from Australia. Her clinical practice is grounded in evidence-based methodologies..

As a compassionate physiotherapist and dedicated learner, Dr.Ajeeta Goel enjoys traveling and reading in her free time. Her areas of interest include chronic pain management, prehabilitation, and recovery. She is eager to take the next step in her career and is committed to making a significant impact as a sports physiotherapist.
